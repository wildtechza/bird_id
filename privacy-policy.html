Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Bird Id - Quiz ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our bird identification quiz application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Bird Id - Quiz is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers.

Local Data Storage

  • Quiz Progress: Your quiz scores and progress are stored locally on your device only
  • App Preferences: Settings such as difficulty level and quiz type preferences are stored locally
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contact lists or calendar information
  • Financial or payment information

How the App Works

Bird Id - Quiz is a fully offline-capable educational quiz application:

  • All bird images, sounds, and data are included in the app installation
  • Quiz questions are generated locally on your device
  • Your scores and progress are stored locally using your device's storage
  • No internet connection is required to use the app (after initial download)
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties

Third-Party Services

Bird Id - Quiz does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely on your device without external dependencies.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected when using Bird Id - Quiz.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Using device lock screen protection (PIN, password, or biometric)
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Being cautious about granting physical access to your device

Changes to App Functionality

If we ever introduce features that collect data differently than described in this policy, we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy and notify users through the app
  • Obtain user consent where required by law
  • Provide clear options to opt-out of data collection

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) do not apply. However, you maintain full control over your data:

  • Delete Your Data: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
  • Reset Progress: You can clear app data through your device settings to reset your quiz progress

App Permissions

Bird Id - Quiz requests minimal permissions:

  • Internet Access: Only used for initial app download and updates (if applicable). The app functions fully offline once installed.
  • Storage: Used to save your quiz progress and preferences locally on your device

International Users

Since Bird Id - Quiz does not collect or transmit data, there are no international data transfer concerns. The app can be used safely from any location worldwide.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Open Source and Transparency

We believe in transparency. The Bird Id - Quiz app is built with open web technologies and does not use obfuscated tracking or data collection code.
